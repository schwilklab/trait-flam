
dry_down_long file:

hour: number of hours since start of dry-down
rep: first digit represents true replication, second digit represents sub-replication
wet_weight: weight of subsample from original saturated litter
dry_weight: weight of subsample after ovendried (48 hours @ 95C
sp: species
MC_wet: moisture content calculated using wet weight
MC_dry: moisture content calculated using dry weight
